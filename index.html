About FUJING Design

富璟設計有限公司，由設計總監王飛馭創立，專注於住宅、商業及辦公空間的整合設計服務。我們相信，好的空間設計不只是視覺美感，更是生活方式的延伸與詮釋。

Founded by Design Director Wang Fei-Yu, FUJING Design specializes in integrated design for residential, commercial, and office spaces. We believe good design extends beyond aesthetics — it embodies a way of living.

空間有限，但想像力無邊。設計不應受制於外在條件，而是放下成見，不斷創造嶄新的感知。
Space is limited, but imagination is limitless. Design should not be restricted by conditions, but rather create brand new perceptions.

What We Offer
Our Services
01
住宅空間設計
Residential Design
Residential Interior
以精準的空間尺度分析與材質選配為核心，無論新成屋規劃或老屋改造，我們系統性地整合動線佈局、採光條件與生活場景，在嚴格的施工品質管理下，交付兼具人體工學與美學張力的居住環境。
Anchored in precise spatial analysis and material curation, we systematically integrate circulation planning, daylighting, and lifestyle scenarios — delivering residences that achieve both ergonomic excellence and aesthetic distinction under rigorous construction oversight.
02
商業空間設計
Commercial Design
Commercial Space
深度解析品牌 DNA 與目標客群行為模式，將視覺識別系統轉譯為空間語彙，從材質肌理、光環境設計到動線規劃，構築具備強烈品牌辨識度與轉化效益的商業場域體驗。
By decoding brand DNA and analyzing target audience behavior, we translate visual identity into spatial vocabulary — crafting commercial environments with compelling brand recognition and measurable conversion impact through materiality, lighting design, and circulation strategy.
03
空間規劃顧問
Space Planning
Consulting
透過系統性的空間效能診斷，評估現有格局、結構條件與使用痛點，提供具備可行性的改善策略與優先序建議，協助業主在預算框架內實現最大化的空間效益與使用品質提升。
Through systematic spatial performance diagnostics, we assess existing layouts, structural conditions, and usage friction points — delivering feasibility-validated improvement strategies and prioritized recommendations that maximize spatial efficiency within defined budget parameters.

Process
從概念到完工，全程陪伴
From Concept to Completion
Design Process
STEP 01
初步諮詢
Consultation
Initial Meeting
瞭解您的需求、預算範圍與空間風格偏好，由設計師親自傾聽您的夢想。
Understanding needs, budget & style preferences through a personal meeting with our designer.
STEP 02
概念設計
Concept Design
Design Proposal
平面規劃、風格意向板、3D示意圖，讓您提前感受未來的空間樣貌。
Floor plan, mood board & 3D visuals, letting you experience the future space in advance.
STEP 03
確認定案
Approval
Final Confirmation
設計修改確認、詳細報價定稿、工程合約正式簽署，確保雙方共識。
Design revisions, detailed quote finalization & formal contract signing to ensure mutual consensus.
STEP 04
施工監管
Construction
Site Management
工班協調排程、定期進度追蹤、現場品質把關，全程透明可查詢。
Contractor coordination, regular progress tracking & quality control — transparent throughout.
STEP 05
竣工交屋
Completion
Handover
現場竣工驗收、詳細交屋說明、完善售後服務，確保每個細節完美。
Final inspection, detailed handover walkthrough & aftercare service for your peace of mind.
